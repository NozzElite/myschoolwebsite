Vision and Mission

Sandpiper School is committed to working with our community to provide an innovative educational program which allows students to meet their academic potential in an environment that inspires creativity and a love of learning. Students will develop a strong work ethic, exhibit high self esteem, learn self management skills and accept responsibility for their actions

Sandpiper is a community of learners in which every person is well known and honored for their individuality. Students conduct themselves with pride, exhibit the highest standard of behavior, and demonstrate personal initiative in their education. Sandpiper works with the community in a spirit of unity and mutual respect to promote excellence in the learning environment. Decisions center on the needs of children to provide a challenging integrated educational program that emphasizes creativity, innovation and flexibility. Graduates possess the skills and knowledge to be successful in an interdependent world. More importantly, we have inspired students to be life long learners.



Principal's Message

Welcome to Sandpiper, a place dedicated to inspirational learning, collaboration, and excellence. It is my great honor to serve as the principal for this wonderful community. I am committed to partnering with each of you to provide an extraordinary experience for our students. The great success of our school is the result of faculty, staff, students, and families coming together in an innovative and caring environment that empowers creativity and critical thinking. Your active support through our parent leadership organizations and your insight as your child's first teacher is essential to our work. Thank you for being involved in your student's education! Last year, we embarked on defining our Profile of a Graduate. That work continues this year as we examine, adopt, and strengthen our signature practices to ensure students leave Sandpiper as culturally aware, empathetic, collaborative, problem seekers and solvers. The work of teaching and learning is invigorating. I am as optimistic and confident as ever that our time together this year as parents, educators, and learners will be a joy.

In partnership,
